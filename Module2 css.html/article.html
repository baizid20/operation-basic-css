Essential Nighttime Skincare: A Quick Guide to Radiant Skin

Your nighttime skincare routine is crucial for repairing and rejuvenating your skin. While you sleep, your body heals and restores, making this the ideal time to boost skin health. Here's a condensed guide to a simple and effective nighttime routine.

Step 1: Cleanse

Start by removing makeup, sunscreen, and impurities accumulated throughout the day. A gentle cleanser does the trick. If you wear heavy makeup, double cleansing with an oil-based cleanser followed by a water-based cleanser is ideal.

Step 2: Exfoliate (2-3 Times a Week)

Exfoliating 2-3 times a week helps remove dead skin cells and unclog pores. Chemical exfoliants with AHAs or BHAs work well. Avoid over-exfoliating to prevent irritation.
